About Mzee

Mzee Kanyamahanga is a master storyteller and keeper of the oral traditions of the Nyanza royal court. Having spent his youth as a royal herdsman, his archive contains rare accounts of the Inyambo cattle rituals and the poetic linguistics of the 1940s.
